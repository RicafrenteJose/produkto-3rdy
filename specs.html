PS5 Technical Specifications

Key hardware specifications for the PlayStation 5 console.

CPU 8x Zen 2 Cores at up to 3.5 GHz (variable frequency)
GPU Custom RDNA 2 with 10.28 TFLOPs, 36 CUs at up to 2.23 GHz (variable frequency)
Memory 16 GB GDDR6 / 256-bit
Memory Bandwidth 448 GB/s
Storage Custom 825 GB SSD
I/O Throughput 5.5 GB/s (raw), typical 8–9 GB/s (compressed)
Expandable Storage M.2 NVMe SSD slot (PCIe Gen4), compatible drives required
External Storage USB HDD support (PS4 games only)
Optical Drive Ultra HD Blu-ray (66G/100G) ~10xCAV, Blu-ray BD-ROM (25G/50G) ~8xCAV
Video Out HDMI 2.1 (4K 120Hz, 8K, VRR)
Audio Tempest 3D AudioTech
Networking Ethernet, Wi‑Fi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.1
USB USB Type‑C, USB Type‑A (various speeds depending on model)
Dimensions (Approx.) Standard: 390 × 104 × 260 mm; Slim: 358 × 96 × 216 mm
Weight (Approx.) Standard: 4.5 kg; Slim: ~3.2 kg
Model Variants PS5 with Ultra HD Blu‑ray disc drive; PS5 Digital Edition
Note: Specifications summarized from official PlayStation communications; check the official product page for the most up‑to‑date details.
